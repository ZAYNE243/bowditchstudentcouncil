Welcome to the Official Website Of Student Council

Bowditch Student Council seeks to create an inclusive environment at school by increasing school spirit, supporting students and staff, and encouraging community service.

What We Do

We put on various events throughout the year such as spirit days, movie nights, the annual Door Decorating Contest and a Toy Drive to benefit the Samaritan House. Spirit Week included themed dress-up days and pep rallies. Student Council reads the announcements daily and gives tours/directions at MAZE Day, Back to School Night and Open House.
